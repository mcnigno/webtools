  ASK BAPCO PROJECT

      Document Naming Engine


The purpose of this application is to identify the types of document used for the BAPCO PROJECT and to define the naming conventions applicable to documents. An overview of the purpose of the different document types, and their role in the Project, is presented. This aims at helping all technical and administrative staff working with BAPCO documents, either as authors, editors, controllers, reviewers or other, to have a common understanding of the use of each document.
